About MedCare Clinic

Learn about our mission, values, and commitment to exceptional healthcare

Our Story

Founded in 2008 by Dr. Sarah Mitchell, MedCare Clinic began as a small family practice with a simple goal: to provide honest, compassionate healthcare to our local community. Over the years, what started as a two-room clinic has grown into a comprehensive healthcare facility serving over 20,000 patients with a wide range of medical services.

Our growth has been driven by a deep commitment to our patients and a belief that quality healthcare should be accessible to everyone. Today, MedCare Clinic is proud to be a trusted healthcare partner for families across the region.
